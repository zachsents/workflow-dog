Privacy Policy for WorkflowDog

Effective Date: April 25, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to WorkflowDog. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services through https://workflow.dog or our associated applications.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Integration Data: We store API keys, OAuth tokens, and other credentials necessary to connect with third-party services.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our services, like creating and managing automations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and maintain your account, to facilitate the creation and management of business automations, and to improve our services through usage data analysis. We also use your information to resolve technical issues and respond to inquiries for customer support.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not directly share your data with service providers. However, your data may pass through the services of providers like Google Cloud and Supabase as part of the service architecture.

5. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of our databases.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@workflow.dog.
